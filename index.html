FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers — if yours isn't here, just get in touch.

How much does a website or app cost?

It depends on scope. A simple website CV starts lower; a full business site or custom app is priced after a free consultation. We always provide a clear quote before any work begins — no surprise invoices.

How long does a typical project take?

A website CV can be ready in 1–2 weeks. Business websites usually take 2–6 weeks. Custom apps vary by complexity — we'll give you an honest timeline in your proposal.

Do you offer ongoing IT support?

Yes. We provide help desk support, device management, backups, security patching, and full IT fleet setup and maintenance — either as a standalone service or alongside a website or app project.

Can you integrate Stripe payments into my site?

Absolutely — Stripe integrations are one of our specialties. We set up checkout, payment links, subscriptions, webhooks, and connect payments to your existing website or app.

What areas do you serve?

We work with businesses remotely across Ireland, the UK, and internationally. Most projects are delivered online with video calls and clear project updates throughout.

How do I get started?

Send us a message via the contact page or email ltdstartek@gmail.com. Tell us what you need and we'll reply within one business day to arrange a free consultation.

Ready to get started?

Tell us about your project — website, CV site, app, payments, or IT support. We'll reply within one business day.
