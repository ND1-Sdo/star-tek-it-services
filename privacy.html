Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Star-Tek IT Services ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or contact us.

Our marketing website is a static site. We do not use analytics cookies or store contact form submissions on our servers. When you use the contact form, your enquiry is sent via your email client directly to us.

If you become a client, we may collect business contact details and project-related information necessary to deliver our services.

How we use information

We may use trusted third-party providers (such as hosting, email, or cloud platforms) to operate our services. We only share data required for those providers to perform their function.

Data retention

We retain client and enquiry records only as long as needed for business, legal, or contractual purposes.

Your rights
